Nicoletta Haddad

Hello and welcome to my little space on the web. I am Nicoletta Haddad and I am a Chiropractic Doctor turned Software Engineer, currently specialising in full-stack web development.

I have 13 years experience in the health industry working in both large establishments such as Westmead Children's Hospital and was the director and owner of my own clinical practice. I love learning and I am always looking for something new to create or discover. I bring my skills as a business owner, problem solver and an empath to the tech industry to extend my reach in helping more people and aid in solving big world problems.

I love plants, putting IKEA furniture together and I make a mean baked cheesecake!
